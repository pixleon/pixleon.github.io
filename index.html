About

Pixleon was founded in 2019 by Jimmie West with the goal to create condensed and challenging, but most important, interesting game experiences.

Pixleon also aims to become an attractive option for organizations in need for dedicated software developers. If you are interested in that please do not hesitate to contact us here.

In 2021 Pixleon released a puzzle game called Marbleous Blocks.
